Privacy Policy for Namma Gobi

Effective Date: July 16, 2025

Namma Gobi (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the mobile application Namma Gobi (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require users to create an account or provide personal information to use the App. The App may collect the following information automatically:

  • Device Information & Identifiers: Advertising ID, device ID, and push notification token for advertising and notification purposes.
  • Approximate Location: Used by ad networks (e.g., AdMob) to deliver relevant advertisements based on region.
  • App Activity: Interactions with the App are collected to improve the user experience and app performance.
  • App Performance Data: Crash logs and diagnostics are collected to identify bugs and improve reliability.

2. Information Provided by Sellers

  • Sellers may submit product details such as product name, category, pricing, description, and images.
  • Sellers may provide their phone number to receive inquiries via WhatsApp.
  • Buyer information is not collected or stored by the App.

3. How We Use the Information

  • To enable push notifications using OneSignal.
  • To show advertisements using Google AdMob.
  • To improve the stability and performance of the App.
  • To enable buyers to contact sellers via WhatsApp.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which may collect or share data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying ads
  • OneSignal — for push notifications
  • Firebase — for database and content storage
  • WhatsApp — for external buyer–seller communication

Users are encouraged to review the individual privacy policies of these services.

5. Data Sharing

Some data (such as device identifiers and approximate location) may be shared with advertising and notification partners to operate core features of the App. We do not sell personal information.

6. Data Retention and Security

We store seller-provided product information securely and use industry-standard security measures to protect user data. Information is encrypted in transit.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Data Deletion Requests

Users or sellers may request removal of their submitted product information or phone number at any time by contacting us at the email provided below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
