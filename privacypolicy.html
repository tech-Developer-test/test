Privacy Policy for S&M

Effective Date: July 16, 2025

S&M (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the mobile application S&M (the “App”). This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information provided by users, especially in connection with WhatsApp-based product enquiries.

1. Information We Collect

  • Seller Phone Numbers: Collected when sellers submit product listings. This is used to generate WhatsApp order links.
  • Product Details: Name, price, category, and image are stored and shown in the app to facilitate user inquiries.
  • We do not collect personal data from buyers (e.g., phone numbers or emails).

2. How We Use the Information

  • Seller phone numbers are used to create WhatsApp buttons for product inquiries.
  • We do not share seller phone numbers with third parties.
  • All WhatsApp messages are sent outside the app, governed by WhatsApp's policies.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses WhatsApp as an external service to enable buyer-seller communication. Please refer to WhatsApp’s own privacy policy: WhatsApp Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

All seller-provided data is stored securely (e.g., via Firebase) and protected from unauthorized access. We do not store or access buyer data.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Your Consent

By submitting listings and using the app, sellers consent to the collection and use of their phone numbers for WhatsApp integration.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. We recommend checking this page for any changes.
